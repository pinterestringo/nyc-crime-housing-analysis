
Data Cleaning Steps:
1. Dropped duplicate rows to avoid redundancy.
2. Removed rows with missing critical data (created_date, borough, complaint_type, zip_code).
3. Renamed columns for better readability (e.g., incident_zip to zip_code).
4. Converted created_date and closed_date to datetime format.
5. Added a new column, time_to_close, to calculate the resolution time in days.
6. Checked for and replaced invalid zip codes using regular expressions.
